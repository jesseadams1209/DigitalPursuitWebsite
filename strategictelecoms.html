BILLIANCE

Billiance is a management platform combining both the Telesa Operations Support Systems (TOSS) and the Telesa Business
Support Systems (TBSS) in support of ITC companies’ need for tools to automate and manage broader aspects of the
environment needed to ensure their viability and reduce their risks.

The platform has been built in a modular architecture allowing for specific deployment that permits historic systems integration. We have
focussed on the six management features needed to sustain a professional billing and provisioning platform that ensures creditability.

TELEGENCE

SUPPLIER MANAGEMENT SERVICE


Telegence serves as a valuable corporate business enabler, including aspects of Vendor Invoice Management and Fault/Discrepancy Management
to support business drivers. This is a secured web-based portal that provides an unrivalled ability to allocate telecoms services and usage costs,
giving a transparent view of the true costs of all your service providers in a holistic view.


TELECOMS COST OPTIMISATION


PROFESSIONAL MANAGEMENT (PM)


PM is a service offering focussed on ensuring that your telecommunication services supports the business
drivers through a broad vendor management approach with a special focus on driving viable service delivery.

This tool plays an active role in supporting service delivery of the business’ telecommunication needs. If required, this
process can extend to an active role in the procurement selection process of procuring vendors and equipment or services.
